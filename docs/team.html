Our Team

Since opening our doors in 1992 we have been treating local Comox Valley residents, as well as those from Denman Island, Hornby Island, and Campbell River.

BScPT, CGIMS, FCAMPT

Kendra Mulligan

Kendra has been offering healing to her clients since graduating from the University of Alberta in 1993. Over the years she has continued to expand and enrich her the skills, experience and knowledge that she is able to offer her clients. Kendra has co-owned Ascent Physiotherapy in Comox, BC since 2010 with Ron Mulligan. One of Kendra's passions is Accupuncture where she has been certified since 2007, but also continuing to advance her knowledge and skills through ongoing training.

Kendra is active in the local mountain biking community and enjoys nature and exploring local, regional and North American mountain biking destinations. The Comox Valley is a perfect fit for Kendra as it also aligns with her farming roots and love of sports in Northern BC.

BScPT

Trish Leslie

Trish graduated from Queen Margaret University in Edinburgh with a Bachelor’s of Science in Physical Therapy, with honours, in 2011. She has worked as a physiotherapist in orthopaedics, women’s health, geriatrics, neurology and sports physiotherapy. Trish has also worked with rugby players at the Leith Rugby Club in Edinburgh and James Bay Rugby Club in Victoria.

Trish has worked in private practice as well as acute care since 2012. Through her practice she focuses on manual therapies, physiotherapy modalities and exercise prescription. Since graduating university she has continued to complete additional courses in orthopaedics and women’s health as well as being certified in acupuncture from Acupuncture Foundation Canada Institute.

Trish grew up in Ucluelet but is happy to have ended up in the Comox Valley and is loving it here.

MPT, FDN/IMS

Shadi Fleifel

Shadi completed his Master’s degree in Physiotherapy in 2008 in Perth, Australia. He has worked with high level sports teams of all kinds throughout his career and is passionate about sports rehabilitation. He has over 10 years of clinical experience, excels in treating sports injuries and “everyday” injuries, including neck and back pain, overuse injuries, and acute musculoskeletal injuries. When not in the office you will find Shadi hard at play with his kids at the park or enjoying a workout. His workout interests include crossfit, swimming, biking and running, rugby, basketball and surfing.
